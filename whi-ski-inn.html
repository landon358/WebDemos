A Boyne Falls Institution

The Whi-Ski Inn is a whiskey-forward BBQ smokehouse in downtown Boyne Falls, just a stone's throw from the Boyne Mountain entrance. As an authentic smokehouse, we treat BBQ as an art form — slowly smoking a variety of meats and sides, with house-made sauces adding layers of flavor to every bite.

Cozy, casual, and family-friendly — about 30 seats inside, a patio outside, and live music on Saturday nights. The kind of local gathering place people call a hidden gem.
